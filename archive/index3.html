Core Orientation / Základná orientácia

EN

VECTAETOS is not an agent, assistant, optimizer, reward system, or decision engine. It is a non-agentic epistemic field framework for describing which configurations of meaning remain structurally coherent without collapse.

SK

VECTAETOS nie je agent, asistent, optimalizátor, systém odmien ani rozhodovací mechanizmus. Je to neagentný epistemický rámec poľa, ktorý opisuje, ktoré konfigurácie významu zostávajú štrukturálne koherentné bez kolapsu.

Architecture / Architektúra

Φ Field / Pole Φ
Primary ontology. A global configuration of relations, not an agent and not a data structure of authority.
Σ₁…Σ₈ Singularities / Ťažiská
Eight invariant axiomatic centers: intent, existence, truth, freedom, unity, reciprocity, wisdom, creation.
Simulation Vortex / Simulačný Vortex
Generates candidate trajectories as projections only. No decision, no optimization, no preferred attractor.
Epistemic Cryptography / Epistemická kryptografia
A read-only audit layer preserving structural coherence and the geometry of uncertainty without intervention.
Projection Layer / Projekčná vrstva
Runes and TetraGlyph describe field states. They are descriptive, non-prescriptive, and non-authoritative.
Human Interpretation / Ľudská interpretácia
Meaning is not imposed by the lower layers. Interpretation remains downstream, explicit, and human-readable.

Entropic Humility / Entropická pokora

EN

Entropic humility means that impulses which would reduce global coherence below κ do not become realizable transitions. They are not forbidden by rule; they fail as ontology.

SK

Entropická pokora znamená, že impulzy, ktoré by znížili globálnu koherenciu pod κ, sa nestanú realizovateľnými prechodmi. Nie sú zakázané pravidlom; zlyhajú ako ontológia.
