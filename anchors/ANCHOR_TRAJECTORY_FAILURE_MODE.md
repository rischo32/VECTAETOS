## 0. Purpose

This anchor defines the formal failure mode of a candidate trajectory in VECTAETOS.

It describes how an impulse or candidate deformation fails to become a realizable possibility.

This is not a safety filter.

This is not a refusal mechanism.

This is not a moral prohibition.

    This is the formal mechanical expression of Entropic Humility:

> destructive, dominating, or eliminative impulses do not arise as realizable possibilities,
> because they fail noetic and ontological representability.
