Epistemic State Symmetry in Vectaetos

Status: Hypothesis

---

1. Purpose of this document

This document records a possible geometric interpretation of the four epistemic states (4ES) used in the Vectaetos architecture.

The hypothesis proposes that the four epistemic states may form a structure equivalent to the cyclic group Z₄.

This interpretation is not part of the canonical ontology and must not be used as an operational rule unless formally derived.

---

2. The four epistemic states (4ES)

Vectaetos defines four epistemic states:

AA
AN
NA
NN

Interpretation:

State| Meaning
AA| coherent for both field and human
AN| meaningful for human, incoherent for field
NA| coherent for field, unintelligible for human
NN| incoherent for both

These states define four epistemic quadrants of interpretation.

Important:

«The states are simultaneous perspectives, not a temporal sequence.»

4. Geometric interpretation

Although no ordering exists, the four states can be arranged geometrically as a square:

AA —— AN
 |      |
NA —— NN

Such a structure admits a rotational symmetry.

The symmetry group of a square includes a cyclic rotation:

Z₄

This motivates the hypothesis:

4ES ≅ Z₄   (geometric interpretation)

7. Relation to QE

QE (Qualitative Epistemic Aporia) occurs when:

no transition Φ → Φ′ preserves K(Φ)

If the Z₄ interpretation were valid, QE could correspond to a blocked rotation in the epistemic square.

This remains speculative.

The Z₄ hypothesis could become relevant if one of the following is proven:

1. K(Φ) is invariant under rotation of epistemic states
2. transitions between states exhibit cyclic symmetry

---

10. Canonical conclusion

Vectaetos does not require Z₄ symmetry.

The four epistemic states form a configuration space, not a rotational process.

Z₄ is recorded here only as a possible emergent symmetry of that space.
