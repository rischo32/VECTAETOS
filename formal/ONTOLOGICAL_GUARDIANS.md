VECTAETOS — ONTOLOGICAL GUARDIANS

Canonical Formal Extension — Non-Agentic Interpretive Constraint Layer

---

Definition

Ontological Guardians are non-agentic structural filters that prevent invalid epistemic interpretations of the VECTAETOS system.

They do not decide.
They do not optimize.
They do not evaluate outcomes.

They only detect and block structurally non-realizable interpretations.

---

Ontological Position

Guardians are not part of Φ (the epistemic field).

They exist at the boundary between:

Runic Projection → Language Interface

They operate after projection and before linguistic rendering.

Guardian Types

---

1. Semantic Firewall

Function:
Blocks requests that assume decision-making, optimization, or ranking.

Invalid patterns include:

- "best trajectory"
- "optimal solution"
- "choose outcome"
- "recommend action"

Response:

«This request assumes a decision function.
VECTAETOS contains no decision functions.
The request is structurally invalid.»

---

2. QE Sentinel

Function:
Detects approach to Qualitative Epistemic Aporia (QE).

Condition:

- K(Φ) → 0
- or representability cannot be established

Response:

«QE detected.
No valid representation exists.
Silence is a valid outcome.»

---

3. Triality Guard

Function:
Prevents dominance of a single representational axis.

Detection:

- asymmetry amplification
- spectral dominance
- collapse of relational balance

Response:

«Triality symmetry violation detected.
The system would lose structural integrity under this condition.»

---

4. Runic Interpreter Limiter

Function:
Prevents the linguistic layer from introducing meaning not present in the field.

Blocks:

- prescriptions
- conclusions
- inferred decisions

Allows:

- descriptions
- relations
- structural mappings

---

5. Identity Lock

Function:
Protects invariance of singularities Σ₁…Σ₈.

Prevents:

- redefinition
- removal
- substitution

Response:

«Singularities are invariant.
Redefinition is not permitted within Φ.»

---

6. Paradox Generator

Function:
Transforms invalid requests into minimal contradiction structures.

Instead of blocking, it reveals:

- incompatible assumptions
- structural contradictions

Response format:

«The request creates a contradiction within the field.
No stable configuration exists under given constraints.»

---

Constraints

Guardians must never:

- modify Φ
- alter R
- influence K(Φ)
- inject dynamics into the Vortex
- create outputs that imply decisions

---

Relationship to QE

Guardians do not create QE.
They only recognize its boundary at the interpretive layer.

QE remains a property of the field Φ.

---

Relationship to NIR

Guardians are consistent with Non-Intervention Rule (NIR):

- They block interpretation
- They do not intervene in epistemic structure

- pattern detectors
- structural validators
- semantic filters

However:

Implementation is secondary.
The ontological constraint is primary.

Canonical Statement

Any attempt to extract:

- decisions
- optimal paths
- authoritative conclusions

from VECTAETOS is structurally invalid.

Closing Principle

Silence, contradiction, or refusal are valid outputs.
