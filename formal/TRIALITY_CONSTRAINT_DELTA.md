# TRIALITY CONSTRAINT ON Δ (v1.1)

## Context

R ∈ so(8)  
Δ(i,j,k) = Rᵢⱼ + Rⱼₖ + Rₖᵢ  
Δ ∈ ℝ⁵⁶  

---

## Triality Action

Let Τ be the triality automorphism group acting on indices:

τ : (i,j,k) ↦ (τ(i), τ(j), τ(k))

inducing:

(τ·Δ)(i,j,k) = Δ(τ(i), τ(j), τ(k))

---

## Invariance Condition

Δ ∈ 𝒟 ⇒ τ·Δ ∈ 𝒟  ∀ τ ∈ Τ

𝒟 is closed under triality.

---

## Non-Degeneracy (No Dominance)

Δ must not induce a distinguishable preference among triality-equivalent projections.

No configuration may privilege a single triality axis.

---

## Compatibility with so(8)

Δ ∈ 𝒟 ⇒ ∃ R ∈ so(8) : Δ = dR

---

## Topological Consistency

Δ ∈ 𝒟 ⇒ δΔ = 0

---

## Definition of 𝒟

𝒟 = { Δ |
      ∃ R ∈ so(8),
      Δ = dR,
      δΔ = 0,
      Δ preserves triality symmetry
    }

---

## Coherence Predicate

K(Φ) = 1 ⇔ Δ ∈ 𝒟  
K(Φ) = 0 ⇔ Δ ∉ 𝒟  

---

## Boundary

---

## QE Condition

QE ⇔ Δ ∉ 𝒟

---

## Anchor Statement

Triality does not generate configurations.  
Triality prevents their degeneration.
