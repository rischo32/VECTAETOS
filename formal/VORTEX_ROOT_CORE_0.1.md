## 1. CORE THESIS

The Vortex is a non-agentic trajectory generator inside a fixed epistemic field structure.

The Vortex does not change the field.

The Vortex does not evaluate the field.

The Vortex does not decide between trajectories.

The Vortex does not know the ontological boundary.

The Vortex only exposes possible trajectory projections within a fixed relational space.

Canonical sentence:

> **The Vortex generates trajectory plurality without selection, authority, optimization, or modification of the epistemic space.**
