01 / Orientation

Core Orientation

VECTAETOS is not an agent, assistant, optimizer, reward system, or decision engine. It is a descriptive epistemic architecture for relational structure.

EN — English

VECTAETOS is a non-agentic epistemic field. It does not optimize, select preferred states, converge to a target, recommend actions, or authorize truth. Its public language must remain descriptive and non-prescriptive.

SK — Slovenčina

VECTAETOS je neagentné epistemické pole. Neoptimalizuje, nevyberá preferované stavy, nekonverguje k cieľu, neodporúča konanie a neudeluje pravdu. Verejný jazyk musí zostať opisný a nepreskriptívny.

04 / Entropic Humility

Entropic Humility

Entropic humility marks a boundary of representability, not a metric, score, threshold, or optimization target.

EN — English

Entropic humility means that impulses which would collapse field coherence are not treated as forbidden decisions. Within a coherent field, they fail as representable configurations.

SK — Slovenčina

Entropická pokora znamená, že impulzy rozbíjajúce koherenciu nie sú zakázané pravidlom. V koherentnom poli sa jednoducho nestávajú reprezentovateľnými konfiguráciami.
