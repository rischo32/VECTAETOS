# VECTAETOS™ - IP STRATEGY

## Status

VECTAETOS is protected primarily through:

- public authorship record,
- DOI timestamping,
- Git history,
- canonical anchors,
- copyright notices,
- defensive publication,
- trademark use.

## Patent Position

VECTAETOS as an ontological framework is not treated as a patent object.

Only concrete technical mechanisms may be evaluated as patent candidates.

## Trademark Position

VECTAETOS™ is used as a source-identifying mark for the project, publications, software, and research artifacts.

Formal registration remains pending.

## Defensive Publication Position

Core ontology, formal anchors, non-agentic constraints, and public architecture are intentionally published as prior art.

## Confidential Position

Unpublished implementation mechanisms may remain confidential until patentability is evaluated.
