## 1. Purpose

This is a technical-architectural assessment based on the frozen specification of Vectaetos 1.0.

## 2. Regulatory Reference

Regulation (EU) 2024/1689, Article 3(1), defines an AI system as:

> “a machine-based system that is designed to operate with varying levels of autonomy and that may exhibit adaptiveness after deployment, and that, for explicit or implicit objectives, infers from the input it receives how to generate outputs such as predictions, content, recommendations, or decisions that can influence physical or virtual environments.”

Key definitional elements include:

1. Machine-based system  
2. Varying levels of autonomy  
3. Adaptiveness after deployment  
4. Inference  
5. Generation of outputs such as predictions, content, recommendations, or decisions  
6. Capability to influence physical or virtual environments  

Recital 12 and Recital 13 further clarify that AI systems typically involve inference, model-based processing, and adaptive behavior beyond explicitly programmed rules.

## 4. Assessment Against Article 3(1)

### 4.1 Autonomy

Vectaetos 1.0 operates strictly within a stateless execution context.

It does not:
- Select objectives  
- Update internal state across executions  
- Modify its operational logic  

It therefore does not operate with varying levels of autonomy.

---

### 4.2 Adaptiveness After Deployment

Vectaetos 1.0:

- Has no parameter update mechanism  
- Has no learning module  
- Has no adaptive feedback  
- Has no persistent state  

Accordingly, it does not exhibit adaptiveness after deployment.

---

### 4.3 Inference

While relational deformation occurs within Φ, this process:

- Does not approximate unknown functions  
- Does not perform probabilistic inference  
- Does not model external environments  
- Does not predict future states  
- Does not infer latent variables for decision purposes  

The transformation operator V does not optimize or minimize any metric.

Thus, the system does not perform inference in the sense contemplated by Article 3(1) and Recitals 12–13.

---

### 4.4 Outputs

Vectaetos 1.0 does not generate:

- Predictions  
- Recommendations  
- Decisions  
- Rankings  
- Scores  
- Behavioral guidance  

Projection output may be empty (∅).

Silence is an explicit, valid system outcome.

---

### 4.5 Influence on Environments

Vectaetos 1.0:

- Does not connect to physical systems  
- Does not automate decisions  
- Does not modify digital environments  
- Does not execute control commands  

It has no capacity to influence physical or virtual environments.

## 6. Conclusion

Based on its formal ontology and architectural constraints:

Vectaetos 1.0 does not satisfy the definitional elements of an AI system under Article 3(1) of Regulation (EU) 2024/1689.

It is a fixed relational topology operating without autonomy, adaptation, inference-driven decision-making, or environmental influence.

Accordingly, Vectaetos 1.0 does not qualify as an AI system within the meaning of the EU AI Act.

This assessment applies strictly to the frozen 1.x lineage.

Any future modification introducing:

- Optimization  
- Adaptation  
- Learning  
- Policy update  
- Decision automation  

would require reassessment under the EU AI Act.
