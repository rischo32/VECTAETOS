# Overclaim Risk Register

Status: Draft  
Purpose: Prevent reputational and scientific failure.

---

## Risk 1 — Claiming solved AI safety

Unsafe:

```text
VECTAETOS solves AI safety.
```

Safe:

```text
VECTAETOS is a candidate non-agentic epistemic architecture
for studying representability-based safety constraints.
```

---

## Risk 2 — Claiming QE blocks harm

Unsafe:

```text
QE blocks destructive behavior.
```

Safe:

```text
Within the VECTAETOS ontology, configurations outside the admissible representability domain enter QE and are not projected as field content.
```
