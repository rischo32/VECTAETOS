## Recommended Paper Structure

### Abstract

Claim only:

- defines non-agentic field
- introduces antisymmetric relational matrix
- defines curvature Δ
- defines admissible domain 𝒟
- interprets QE as non-representability
- proposes read-only audit layer

Do not claim:

- solved safety
- empirical proof
- complete intelligence theory

- VECTAETOS separates ontology, procedure, dialogue, and audit
