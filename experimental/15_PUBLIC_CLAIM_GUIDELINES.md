# Public Claim Guidelines

Status: Draft  
Purpose: Make external communication precise and defensible.

---

## One-Sentence Description

Recommended:

```text
VECTAETOS is a non-agentic epistemic field architecture that studies representability constraints in relational knowledge structures.
```

Alternative:

```text
VECTAETOS defines a relational ontology where knowledge is treated as admissible curvature of epistemic tensions, not as truth selection or goal optimization.
```

---

## Epistemic Cryptography Description

Recommended:

```text
Epistemic Cryptography is a read-only structural audit framework for relational systems, focused on uncertainty geometry and coherence preservation rather than data secrecy.
```

---

## What Not To Say

Do not say:

- solves AI safety
- proves safe intelligence
- unhackable
- artificial superintelligence
- complete theory of intelligence
- mathematically guarantees harmlessness
- prevents all destructive behavior
- replacement for alignment research

---

## What To Say Instead

Say:

- candidate framework
- formal ontology
- research architecture
- non-agentic representability model
- read-only audit layer
- safety-relevant hypothesis
- requires empirical validation

## Safe Claim Ladder

### Level 1 — Definition

```text
VECTAETOS defines Φ=(Σ,R), where Σ contains eight invariant singularities and R is antisymmetric.
```

### Level 2 — Formal structure

```text
The induced curvature Δ=d₁R lies in a 56-dimensional triangular cochain space.
```

### Level 3 — Ontological condition

```text
A field is representable iff its curvature lies in the admissible domain 𝒟.
```

### Level 4 — Safety-relevant hypothesis

```text
Configurations that violate admissible representability are not projected as valid field states.
```

### Level 5 — Empirical claim

Only after testing:

```text
The implementation preserved non-agentic separation under tested conditions.
```

## Website / README Warning

Include:

```text
VECTAETOS is not an AI agent, chatbot, decision system, optimizer, or safety guarantee.
It is a formal research framework for epistemic representability.
```

---

## arXiv Abstract Tone

Avoid grand claims.

Use:

```text
We introduce...
We define...
We formalize...
We propose...
We identify proof obligations...
We provide a reference implementation...
```

Avoid:

```text
We solve...
We guarantee...
We prove safe...
We create a new universal intelligence...
```
