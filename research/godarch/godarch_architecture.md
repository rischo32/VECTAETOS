VECTAETOS už explicitne zakazuje autoritu, optimalizáciu, agentnosť, spätné väzby a dominantný pól. Jadro hovorí, že VECTAETOS nie je agent, rozhodovací systém, optimalizačný mechanizmus ani autoritatívna entita; je to topológia invariantných singularít a relačných napätí bez intervencie.

Preto „GodArch“ nesmie znamenať:

superagent,
riadiacu vrstvu,
centrálny mozog,
najvyšší rozhodovací modul,
božskú interpretáciu reality.

Správny význam je opačný:

GodArch je architektúra, ktorá znemožňuje, aby sa akákoľvek vrstva systému vyhlásila za Boha.

# GodArch

## Definition

GodArch is a non-authoritative meta-architectural constraint layer for epistemic systems.

It does not decide, optimize, interpret, command, rank, or produce truth.

Its sole function is to detect architectural conditions under which a system, model, projection, interpreter, institution, or user begins to claim final epistemic authority.

GodArch is not a divine architecture.

It is an architecture against epistemic divinization.

## Core Principle

No component may become God.

No projection may become truth.

No interpretation may become authority.

No audit may become control.

No memory may become will.

No coherence predicate may become a moral verdict.

## Relation to VECTAETOS

GodArch is compatible with VECTAETOS only insofar as it preserves:

- zero agency,
- non-intervention,
- no optimization,
- no feedback loops,
- intrinsic humility,
- silence legitimacy,
- non-authoritative interpretation.

Any implementation of GodArch that introduces command, preference, optimization, hierarchy, or final truth ceases to be GodArch.

## Ontological Status

GodArch is not a module inside the epistemic field Φ.

It is a meta-architectural safeguard around implementations that claim relation to VECTAETOS or similar non-agentic epistemic frameworks.
