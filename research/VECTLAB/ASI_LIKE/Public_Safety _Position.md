## Public Safety Position

The foundational development of VECTAETOS™ may continue at the level of ontology,
formalization, documentation, and safety verification.

However, the legitimization, expansion, or deployment of higher operative layers
remains intentionally suspended.

Neither ASIMULATOR™ nor ASI_MOD™ will be framed, released, or treated
as validated higher-order operative architecture
until all foundational layers L0–L4 are completed,
empirically tested,
and subjected to adversarial evaluation of Φ.

If this architecture ever approaches ASI-like relevance,
its transition beyond foundational research
must not become the decision of a single author.

Such a transition would require
an explicit multi-party safety deliberation,
conducted at a common round table
with all materially relevant and competent stakeholders invited,
including independent safety researchers,
technical experts,
affected institutions,
and interested industrial actors.

The question is not who can build it first,
but under what shared conditions it may ever become legitimate to proceed.

Until such conditions exist,
no unilateral claim of readiness,
authority,
or deployment legitimacy will be made.
