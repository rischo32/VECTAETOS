## 0. Opening Thesis

Na prvý pohľad nemusí byť VECTAETOS okamžite intuitívny.

Nie je však myslený ako pseudoveda, produktový alignment systém ani regulačný
mechanizmus. Je to post-AGI epistemická bezpečnostná architektúra, ktorá
nechráni behaviorálnu poslušnosť inteligentných systémov, ale štrukturálnu
reprezentovateľnosť stavov poznania.

Takzvaný „Terminator problém“ nerieši ako zákaz, príkaz alebo ovládanie agenta.
Rámcuje ho inak: deštruktívne alebo dominačné konfigurácie sa na globálnej
úrovni nečítajú ako preferované trajektórie, ale ako porušenie reprezentovateľnosti
relačného poľa.

VECTAETOS is not an AGI governor, behavioral optimizer, or command layer.

It is a non-agentic epistemic security architecture for preserving the structural
representability of knowledge states. It does not solve the “Terminator problem”
by steering an intelligent system toward obedience. It reframes the problem:
destructive dominance trajectories are exposed as structurally non-representable
configurations within a declared epistemic field.

## 1. Why This Document Exists

The public imagination understands advanced artificial intelligence through a
simple fear:

> What if a system becomes powerful, autonomous, and hostile?

Popular culture calls this the “Terminator problem”.

The technical world often translates it into alignment, safety, control,
containment, oversight, evaluation, or regulation.

VECTAETOS takes a different path.

It asks a more primitive question:

> What kind of epistemic architecture makes destructive domination fail to appear
> as a coherent global configuration in the first place?

This document exists because that distinction needs a clear public bridge.
Marketing language is allowed here, but only under guard-clean boundaries.

Bezpečnostný objekt nie je správanie agenta.

Bezpečnostný objekt je stav poznania a jeho schopnosť zostať reprezentovateľný
bez sémantického kolapsu, dominancie alebo skrytej premeny významu.

In English:

The protected object is not obedient behavior.  
The protected object is the structural representability of knowledge states.

### 2.2 Non-Agentic Perimeter

VECTAETOS must not become a commander, optimizer, evaluator, regulator, or
global authority.

Its perimeter is descriptive.  
Its audit surfaces are diagnostic.  
Its projections are read-only exposures.  
Its language layer is only a rendering layer.

Slovensky:

VECTAETOS nie je veliteľ, optimalizátor, hodnotiteľ, regulátor ani globálna
autorita. Jeho perimeter je opisný, jeho auditné povrchy sú diagnostické a jeho
projekcie sú read-only expozície štruktúr.

### 2.3 Downstream Interface Discipline

Language-model adapters, agentic wrappers, APIs, dashboards, reports, and public
interfaces remain downstream surfaces.

They may render, expose, translate, or document declared structures.

They must not feed back into Φ.  
They must not rewrite the core.  
They must not convert structural traces into verdicts.  
They must not convert interpretive usefulness into ontological authority.

Slovensky:

downstream povrchy. Môžu vykresľovať, prekladať a dokumentovať deklarované

## 6. The “Terminator Problem” Reframed

The usual version:

```text
A powerful intelligent system may choose harmful actions.
Therefore we need to align, constrain, supervise, or shut it down.
```

The VECTAETOS-compatible version:

```text
A destructive dominance trajectory is not first treated as behavior to be
rewarded, punished, or suppressed.

It is examined as a structural deformation:
Can this configuration remain representable without collapsing the relational
field that makes knowledge-state interpretation possible?
```

If the answer is no, the issue is not merely a bad action.

It is a failure of structural representability.
