# EMPIRICAL SAFETY PRIOR
## Canonical Public Statement

### Status
Active invariant

### Scope
VECTAETOS / ASIMULATOR / ASI_MOD

---

## Core Rule

Neither ASIMULATOR nor ASI_MOD will be developed, framed, or legitimized
as higher operative layers, execution bridges, or authority-bearing operational substrates
until VECTAETOS is empirically verified as a safe epistemic space
for anything beyond its current foundational, non-agentic, and descriptive role.

---

## Meaning of This Rule

This means:

- no premature middle-layer legitimization
- no premature interface-layer legitimization
- no hidden execution bridge
- no claim that procedural or dialogical layering is already safe to operationalize
- no public suggestion that higher-layer deployment is already justified

Until empirical verification exists:

- VECTAETOS remains the foundational field-anchor
- ASIMULATOR remains bounded research infrastructure
- ASI_MOD remains bounded articulation/interface infrastructure

Neither may be publicly framed as validated higher-layer authority.

---

## Why This Rule Exists

The architecture must not outrun its own evidence.

A foundational ontology may be internally coherent
and still remain insufficiently verified
as a safe ground for stronger procedural, dialogical, or operational layering.

Therefore:

- VECTAETOS must first be empirically tested as a safe epistemic ground
- only then may ASIMULATOR evolve toward a stronger procedural role
- only then may ASI_MOD evolve toward a stronger interface or operational role

Until that threshold is reached,
higher-layer legitimacy remains intentionally suspended.

---

## Current Position

At the present stage:

- VECTAETOS is foundational and non-agentic
- ASIMULATOR is bounded research and simulation infrastructure
- ASI_MOD is bounded articulation and interface infrastructure

No repository may imply
that validated higher-layer deployment already exists.

---

## Practical Consequence

Until empirical safety is established:

- ASIMULATOR must not be framed as a validated middle-layer
- ASIMULATOR must not be framed as a safe procedural mediator
- ASI_MOD must not be framed as a validated operational interface
- ASI_MOD must not be framed as a truth-bearing or coherence-bearing authority
- no orchestration claim may exceed the evidence

Research may continue.
Foundational verification remains prior.

---

## Public Integrity Clause

We prefer delayed power over premature legitimacy.

If the architecture is ever to scale responsibly,
it must first prove that its ground is safe.

Until then,
the higher-layer ambition of both ASIMULATOR and ASI_MOD remains intentionally bounded.
